Meet Michael

Though not of Asian descent, Michael's extensive travels throughout Japan, Vietnam, and Thailand have deeply influenced his understanding and appreciation of these vibrant cuisines. His journey began with a simple love for food, which evolved into a lifelong quest to master the art of Asian cooking.

After years of studying and collaborating with local chefs from various Asian cultures, Michael opened Ezo Kitchen in the suburbs of Long Island, with the vision of creating a space where people could experience authentic Asian flavors in a welcoming environment. His philosophy centers on cultural respect and authenticity, ensuring that every dish on the menu honors the traditions from which it originates.

At Ezo Kitchen, we prioritize community and education, gradually introducing guests to the diverse flavors of sushi while also showcasing beloved Vietnamese and Thai dishes. Our mission is to not only provide exceptional dining experiences but also to foster a deeper understanding and appreciation of Asian culinary arts.
